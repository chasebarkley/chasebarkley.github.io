COVID-19 RESPONSE

Update: March 19, 2020

Starting at 5 P.M. our dining room will be closed. We will no longer be offering consumption of food or drink on premise.

All events have been cancelled until April 5, 2020.

We will still be offering curb side pick up and take out orders.
Update: March 17, 2020

At Legghorn's, we are committed to the well being and health of our guests and team.

We are offering curbside pickup until further notice, call 256-857-7181 to order, then call again and your food will be delivered to your vehicle.

Guests will be seated at a safe distance apart if they choose to dine in.

To help mitigate the virus we are no longer offering buffet during lunch. If necessary we will close our dining room and offer carry out. We subsequently will be prepared to close until further notice.

We will keep this page updated and continue to update on social media.

Thank you.
